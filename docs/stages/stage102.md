# Stage102 - Real Writer Trial & Blind Benchmark

Stage102 validates Stage101 through deterministic writer-facing evidence.

It compares direct provider-style baselines with V1700 prose, V1700 scenario,
V430 scenario-room fixture, and V1700 hybrid workflow candidates. The benchmark
is local and reproducible. It does not claim external human market validation.

## Steps

- Stage102.0: Preflight and Trial Scope Lock
- Stage102.1: Real Writer Workflow Trial
- Stage102.2: Blind Benchmark
- Stage102.3: Revision Efficiency and Continuity Audit

## Release Blockers

- Stage101 baseline fails.
- Mandatory pre-development protocol is absent.
- Writer task completion is incomplete.
- Blind candidate count is below 8.
- V1700 family does not win the benchmark.
- V1700 margin over pure GPT direct baseline is below 0.5.
- Revision time reduction is below 30 percent.
- Issue reduction is below 50 percent.
- Provider calls, raw manuscript leakage, or Node2 raw reveal access are nonzero.
- External human validation is falsely claimed.
